Welcome to Aethera
As you start this adventure, remember that you are not alone. One of you, Eben, will venture into a realm of wonder: Aethera, while the other embodies the spirit of Aurora, his departed beloved, guiding him as a petite fairy. This is a cooperative experience that symbolizes the phases of grief, mirroring the emotional journey of those who have loved and lost. From denial to anger, depression, and, eventually, the path to rebuilding, you will traverse these stages together.
To communicate, Eben must interact with the in-game world, while Aurora will provide guidance through dialogues and actions on the phone. For the player emboding Aurora, messages will appear on the screen in blue boxes, you have to read them aloud to inform Eben about the situation.
Until the first blue box, Aurora should refrain from speaking aloud !
When you feel ready to embark on this journey, press the button to enter the adventure.
